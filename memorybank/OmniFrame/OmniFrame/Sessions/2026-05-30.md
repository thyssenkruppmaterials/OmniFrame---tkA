## Key conclusions

  rust_decimal/utoipa; tower-http is 0.5 fleet-wide (not 0.6) so no version-alignment
  work needed for utoipa/otel.

  is a hand-maintained mirror of the Rust struct (drift risk).
- Dashboard serde = snake_case (clean for utoipa); core-service = camelCase (breakage
  risk for any core codegen).
- utoipa additive/safe but low payoff for dashboard (1 endpoint); core-service is the
  higher-ROI target.
